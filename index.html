About Smallcase

A smallcase is a basket of stocks that are intelligently weighted to reflect an idea, theme or strategy. For example, the Rising Rural Demand smallcase is a portfolio of companies that stand to benefit from rising rural consumption.

 

Created by experts registered with SEBI, smallcases help investor reduce risk as a result of well-diversified portfolios. Unlike mutual funds, smallcases are transparent and give you complete control as the stocks are deposited in your demat account. Further, there are no lock-ins or management fees.

 

Find multiple ideas and invest in 2-clicks. smallcases are available through India’s leading brokers - Zerodha, HDFC securities, Kotak securities, Axis Direct, Edelweiss and 5paisa.
